LOCATION AWARENESS
WITH BLUETOOTH LE

Redar™ Beacon (Bluetooth LE) is a new class of low-power, low-cost transmitter technology that can notify you of nearby mobile devices.

The combination of Redar™ Beacon with Redar™ app provides instant location awareness that enables you to customize any of the following scenarios:

  • Welcome entering or nearby passing customers to your establishment with a special offer.

  • Greet them and provide suggestions as they come in.

  • Thank them for their business and send them an offer for their next visit.
